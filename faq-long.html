Getting started

What is Link Exchange?

"It’s like Google AdSense for Token-based Communities". You can add links directly to a widget, boost its visibility within a ranked feed, and see who's bidding for your attention. For a complete description of the service, click here.

What's Metamask?

MetaMask is a bridge that allows you to visit the distributed web of tomorrow in your browser today. It allows you to run Ethereum dApps directly in your browser without running a full Ethereum node.

Why is my Metamask locked?

MetaMask automatically locks your account after a certain period of time. To unlock MetaMask, click the MetaMask extension and enter your password in the field provided.

How do I receive a particular token?

It depends on the token, as each one is different. We recommend contacting the token's publisher to receive the most accurate information. Alternatively, check the exchanges on coinmarketcap.com

Basic functionality

Can I add my own link?

Yes! All you need to do is click the button 'Add your link' and follow the steps. Once you've completed the steps and paid the initial fee (as defined by the token's publisher), your link will be visible when the link is approved.

My link is still not visible in the widget. What's worng?

Don't panic! There might be few reasons:


a) Your transaction is still pending. Please check the status and see if your link is already visible to the publisher.
b) The publisher has not accepted your request. To find out why, contact the publisher directly.

The publisher did not accept my request. Can I get a refund?

Unfortunately not. The publisher specifies what types of links are accepted during the link creation process, and it is the link creator's responsibility to follow them.

My link has been accepted, but I can't see it on the website!

Most likely, your link is not boosted and is outside of the slots. Just add some more tokens to it and watch it grow! :)

Can I create my own widget?

No problem. Creating a widget can be done in minutes. For more information, click here.

Payment model

Why am I paying per probability?

Each probability is your link's chance of being displayed in a publisher's widget. Each link in a slot competes with other links, based on an algorithm score.

How's the probability score calculated?

It depends on each widget's algorithm, as the widget owner can create custom ones.

For example, the Links algorithm sorts links according to this formula:

period = 7 days score = Sum( if now - claim.create_at > period: 0 else claim.value / period * (now - claim.create_at) )

Depending on the expected traffic, the widget’s expiration date and the publisher’s reputation, sponsors can adjust the probability score of their links by sending tokens to the widget.

Can I rank links with my own custom algorithm?

Of course! You can develop your own algorithm and use it in your widget however you like.
