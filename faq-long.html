What's Metamask?

MetaMask is a bridge that allows you to visit the distributed web of tomorrow in your browser today. It allows you to run Ethereum dApps right in your browser without running a full Ethereum node.

Why is Metamask locked?

MetaMask automatically locks your account after a certain period of time. Click on the MetaMask extension and type in your password.

How to receive particular token?

It depends. It's best to ask publisher or simply check the exchanges on coinmarketcap.com

Basic functionality

Can I add my own link?

Yes! All you need to do is click the button 'Add your link' and go through the process. Once you're done and paid initial fee defined by publisher you need to wait until your link's accepted to be visible.

My link's still not visible in the widget. What's happening?

Nothing to worry about! There might be few reasons:


a) Your transaction is still pending. Please check the status and see if your link is already visible to the publisher.
b) Publisher still did not accept your request, it would be best to contact him.

Publisher did not accept my request. Can I get a refund?

Unfortunately, no. Publisher defines initial fee as a form of handshake, usually it's clearly defined what types of links are expected in a widget as well.

My link is accepted, however I can't see it on the website!

It's probably because your link is not boosted and it's outside of the slots. Just add some tokens to it and watch it grow! :)
